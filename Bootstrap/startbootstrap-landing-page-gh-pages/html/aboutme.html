Hawaii Trip

During my most recent trip to Hawaii in Novemeber, I got the opportunity to spend time with my family. The time spent with them involved a beautiful sunrise hike up toDiamond Head up to the top of the summit watching the sunrise over the ocean. After the sunrise hike another memorable part of visiting the island of Oahu was enjoying the beautiful Makaha Beachwhere surfers and body boarders enjoy the large swells and the warm sunshine. My time in Hawaii spending time with family, connecting back to my culture, and making memorable moments and memories with my family at these beautiful destinations was truly incredible.

The time spent in Hawaii was incredible and listed below are some beautiful beaches and activities to do while on Oahu. Along with delicious food that you can't pass up on while in Hawaii.

    • Makaha Beach
    • Diamond Head Summit
    • White Plains Beach
    • Electric Beach
    • Honolulu Stadium Swap Meet
    • Polynesian Cultural Center
